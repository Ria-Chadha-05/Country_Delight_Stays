Customer Understanding

Mid & Premium Segment — Needs & Expectations

01 — Segment Overview

Who each segment is

Mid

₹3,000–5,000 / person / day · Age 25–40

Who: Young professionals, families, friend groups
Trip style: Day trip to 2 nights, weekend-driven
Buying mindset: Value-conscious, wants curation but still price-aware

Premium

₹10,000+ / person / day · Age 30–45

Who: Couples, senior couples, celebration occasions
Trip style: 1–2 nights, occasion or comfort-driven
Buying mindset: Pays to remove effort and get exclusivity, not to save money
02 — Mid Segment: Needs & Expectations

What has to be true for a Mid customer to book

NeedExpectation
TrustVetted, pre-checked options — not a raw listing to research themselves
Price transparencyAll-in pricing upfront, no hidden costs at checkout
Low planning effortPre-built, bookable itinerary instead of open-ended search
Family/pet fitExplicit filtering — a wrong-fit option is disqualifying, not just less appealing
Value justificationClear reason the price beats doing it themselves
03 — Premium Segment: Needs & Expectations

What has to be true for a Premium customer to book

NeedExpectation
Zero planning effortFully bundled end-to-end, including transport — no decisions left to make
Privacy/exclusivityNot a crowded, generic resort — private or curated-scarce venues
PersonalizationCustomization for occasions (anniversaries, celebrations)
DistinctivenessShareable, non-generic experience — not "every other resort on Instagram"
Service qualityWhite-glove handling; price is not the deciding factor
